Intro
To anyone interested in UFOs for any reason this site is for you.  
At this point real or not, UFOs are part of our culture, to the point 
that the FBI recently released a report on the subject.  My personal 
interest comes from my grandfather, who in 1957 reported he saw a flying 
formation of UFOs while hunting in Western Colorado. Then a travel-stop 
in Roswell New Mexico told me there is a whole industry based on this 
hobby/scientific interest.  

Grandpa Burt
